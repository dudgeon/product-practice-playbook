## What it is

A cross-team effort to make AI-augmented product work legible: to collect what’s working, organize it against a shared lifecycle, and lower the cost of adopting a good pattern from "rebuild it yourself" to "read this and fork it".

## How to take part

Every section of the playbook has an "add to this section" link, and the header carries a submit button. Contributions are GitHub issues — low-ceremony, public, and easy to discuss before anything is merged.
