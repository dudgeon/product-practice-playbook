## Why I keep this

I kept watching teams reinvent the same AI-augmented patterns in isolation — and watching good patterns die because nobody wrote them down. The playbook is the durable place those patterns live, organized so you can find the one that fits where you actually are in the work.

## How I curate

I read every submission, place it on the spine, and write the editor’s notes. I try to be generous about what counts as practice and conservative about what gets endorsed. When I’m editorializing, I say so.
