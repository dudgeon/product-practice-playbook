## Goal

Converting interview transcripts into a coherent PRD took 3–4 days. By the time the doc landed, the freshness of the conversations was gone.

## Approach

A skill ingests a folder of .vtt transcripts and drafts a PRD: problem statement, jobs-to-be-done, prioritized requirements, and open questions — each claim footnoted back to a transcript timecode.

## Impact

One PM cleared a backlog of 6 PRDs in an afternoon. Eng leads said the traceability changed how much they trusted the doc.
