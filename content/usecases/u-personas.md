## Goal

Persona work was either thin (invented in a room) or stale (built once, never refreshed).

## Approach

An agent clusters interview utterances into latent personas with traceability to source. Personas live as markdown files with a recency metric; a monthly run shows which are drifting.

## Impact

Personas now visibly used in 4 product reviews — trusted because the source utterances are one click away.
