## Goal

Async design reviews collected sparse, low-signal comments. Reviewers said the open-ended format was the problem.

## Approach

A skill turns the design artifact into a structured review template with prompts at the right specificity — clarity, hierarchy, accessibility, content — then synthesizes responses into a single thread.

## Impact

Reviewer engagement, measured by words-per-review, tripled. Designers rate the feedback meaningfully higher.
