## Goal

Most prompt libraries become folders of dead snippets within three months.

## Approach

Every prompt carries three required fields: a use case, a known failure mode, and a freshness date. Anything 90 days stale is auto-archived. The failure-mode field is what drives quality.

## Impact

Three quarters in: 142 active prompts, 80+ used at least monthly, fewer than 10 stale.
