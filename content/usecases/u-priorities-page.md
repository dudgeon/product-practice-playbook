## Goal

Spec-drafting and triage agents ranked work using whatever strategy fragments their context happened to contain — usually last quarter's deck. PMs then spent review time re-litigating priorities that had already been decided, and nobody could say which version of the strategy an agent had obeyed.

## Approach

The quarterly priorities and the standing decision principles moved to one versioned Markdown page at a stable URL — each priority dated and owned, with a one-line rationale and an explicit expiry. Team agents are instructed to ground ranking and triage calls on the page and cite the entry they used; reviews check the citations instead of re-arguing the strategy. Editing the page is the act of deciding.

## Impact

Spec rework traced to direction drift fell about 60%, and the "is this still a priority?" threads largely disappeared. Two product leaders now draft directly into the page rather than into slides.
